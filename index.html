About us

This webpage introduces you to five Computing female students, currently in their 3rd year at The African Leadership University. Their individual pages would provide you with rich details on their latest internship experiences based in different countries accross the African continent.

Their diverse internship experiences will provide you with insights on the different types of experiences you are likely to get as a future intern.
